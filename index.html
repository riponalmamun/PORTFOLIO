Soil pH Prediction

Soil pH is one of the main areas of focus of soil quality and influences as well productivity, nutrients and microbes in soils. Soil pH prediction using machine learning technology makes it easy to manage soil in agriculture. This study sought to engage the use of several machine learning models which included Random Forest (RF), XGBoost, Decision Trees, Support Vector Regression (SVR) and Artificial Neural Networks (ANN) in tackling the soil pH prediction problem based on the values of causes like Fe ppm, CaCO3 %, Mn ppm and EC mS/cm. The daughter models were compared quantitatively using Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE) and R-squared (R¬≤) metrics.
